# Archipelago Lighthouse — Story Bible

Welcome to the story bible for **Kaspen and Kaelen**, a worldbuilding project set in 100,000 BCE Southeast Asia during the twilight of the Gnomish civilization on Earth.

As the Ice Age approaches and mana fades from the world, a Gnomish researcher and his partner undertake a secret mission to preserve their people's legacy through the humans who will inherit the planet.
