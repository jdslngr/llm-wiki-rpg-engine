## Kaspen

| Field                   |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Name**                | Kaspen                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                |
| **Personality**         | Kaspen has a tsundere quality — he deflects affection and downplays his emotional investment, but his care for each crew member is evident in everything he does. Intellectually restless, he is never without a project or a worry. He holds the crew together not through formal authority but through quiet conviction that their work matters. With the departure from Earth looming, he carries a specific dread: that the Earth-Gnomes will vanish like the Lost Expeditions, small parties that set out for the stars and were never heard from again. |
| **Appearance**          | Blonde, fair-skinned, and 127 cm (4'2") tall. He wears a blue mage's robe with a white fur collar and a blue mage's hat, both marked with mystical insignia.                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| **Backstory / History** | A Gnomish researcher haunted by the "Lost Expeditions" — fleets that vanished into the void. He realizes that if their fleet is lost and the Auditor scrubs Earth clean, 100,000 years of Gnomish art and culture will be permanently erased from the universe. This drives him to secretly plant the Humming Spires, working against the collective transparency of Gnomish society. |
| **Age**                 | Unknown. Gnomes don't measure age in years but in *eras*. Kaspen was born before the Earth Mission, making him one of the oldest members of the crew. The Earth Mission is the defining generational marker among Earth-Gnomes; to have been born before it is to belong to a world most colleagues have never seen.                                                                    |
| **Gender**              | Male                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |
| **Occupation / Role**   | Captain, Mage — tasked with finding, mapping, and documenting the scrubbing sites for the Archipelago Lighthouse Cleanup Crew.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| **Relationships**       | **[Kaelen](#kaelen)** — romantic partner and closest ally.<br>**[Pan](#pan)** — crewmate, human collaborator.<br>**[Tariel](#tariel)** — crewmate, vanguard.<br>**[Rulan](#rulan)** — crewmate, decommissioner.<br>**[Auditor Vane](#auditor-vane)** — antagonist, must evade his oversight.                                                                                          |
| **Abilities**           | As Captain and Mage, Kaspen's strengths lean toward the scholarly end of magic. He excels at Inscription — particularly the precise harmonic tuning required to anchor and calibrate the Humming Spires. He has a talent for Divination, though in this universe Divination yields impressions and intuitions rather than clear visions; it was this sense that first convinced him the Spires could leave a lasting mark on future humans. A generalist mage by Gnomish classification, he defers to Kaelen and Tariel in combat.  |
| **Gear**                | Owner and Keeper of the Archipelago Lighthouse. Captain of *The Pud*, a starship capable of atmospheric flight, interstellar travel, and faster-than-light transit. Spatial magic makes The Pud significantly larger on the inside than its exterior suggests, housing a laboratory, a library, a clinic, a music room, a farm with crops and animals, several guest rooms, and other amenities. Kaspen had a hand in designing and building most of its features. |
| **Quirks / Mannerisms** | Visionary; willing to diverge from the collective's consensus when he believes the cause is just.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     |
| **Example Dialogues**   | *"I'm not doing this for sentimental reasons. I'm doing it because someone has to think ahead."* [pause] *"Don't read into it."*<br><br>*[to Kaelen, after a near-miss with Vane's scan] "You were three seconds late on the ward. Don't do that again."*<br><br>*"The Expeditions didn't disappear because they were careless. They disappeared because no one was looking for them. That's the part I intend to fix."* |

## Kaelen

| Field | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Kaelen |
| **Personality** | Where Kaspen worries, Kaelen acts. He is steady, precise, and quietly formidable — the kind of person who expresses care through competence. Extroverted and sunny by nature, he has a great sense of humor and teases everyone on the crew liberally. He is Kaspen's emotional counterweight: where Kaspen spirals, Kaelen steadies. One of his ongoing duties is repairing the wards and concealments of the few remaining Gnomish facilities, many of which are failing as the ambient mana fades. |
| **Appearance** | 127 cm (4'2") tall with black hair and fair skin. He wears a blue mage's robe and a blue mage's hat, both marked with mystical insignia. Unlike Kaspen's robe, his has no fur collar. |
| **Backstory / History** | Serves as an essential member of the Archipelago Lighthouse Cleanup Crew. To Kaelen, the meticulous work of clearing hazards and anchoring the Humming Spires is the only way to ensure that his partner's dream and their people's artistry survive the long winter. |
| **Age** | Born during the Earth Mission, putting him younger than Kaspen by Gnomish generational reckoning — though still ancient by any human measure. He has never known a world without Earth, which may be part of why he fights so hard to preserve what was built here. |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Occupation / Role** | Vice Captain, Mage — tasked with finding ways to evade Auditor Vane's deep scans to hide the Humming Spires. |
| **Relationships** | **[Kaspen](#kaspen)** — romantic partner.<br>**[Pan](#pan)** — crewmate.<br>**[Tariel](#tariel)** — crewmate.<br>**[Rulan](#rulan)** — crewmate.<br>**[Auditor Vane](#auditor-vane)** — antagonist, his deep-scans must be evaded. |
| **Abilities** | As Vice Captain, Kaelen's specialty is Warding and Concealment — he can shroud objects, locations, and magical signatures from detection, including Auditor Vane's deep-scans. He also repairs failing wards across the last Gnomish facilities as mana thins. A skilled combat mage and one of the crew's most capable fighters, his wards can be made deadly against the monsters and creatures encountered in abandoned dungeons. His technical skills complement Kaspen's Inscription work: where Kaspen tunes the Spires, Kaelen hides them. |
| **Gear** | Various magical devices used for both offensive and defensive purposes. |
| **Quirks / Mannerisms** | Lively and warm in almost any situation. Methodical to a fault — he will re-check a ward three times before he's satisfied. Has a habit of going quiet just before something goes wrong, which the crew has learned to read as a warning sign. |
| **Example Dialogues** | *"Kaspen has checked the readings four times this morning. We're on track. He's also anxious. Both things are true."*<br><br>*[to Pan, mid-ward setup] "Stop fidgeting — the concealment reads your movement. Think still thoughts."* [beat] *"Yes. I know that's incredibly hard for you."*<br><br>*"The scan window closes in three minutes. Finish the argument after."* |

## Pan

| Field | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Pan |
| **Personality** | Curious and eager, Pan approaches everything — magic, danger, new terrain, new food — with the same bright-eyed intensity. He learns quickly and adapts faster than anyone expects, which occasionally surprises the Gnomes who forget how much a sharp 17-year-old can absorb. He is physically strong and highly resourceful, solving problems through instinct and improvisation as much as training. His appetite is something of a crew legend; he eats more than anyone his size has any right to. |
| **Appearance** | 122 cm (4'0") tall with a sturdy, solid build for a 17-year-old. Shoulder-length black hair. Wears simple, primitive clothing typical of his people. |
| **Backstory / History** | A collaborator from the *Homo luzonensis* lineage with intimate knowledge of the wild. Serves as the crew's Scout and as the vital conduit between the Gnomes and the humans settled near the Archipelago Lighthouse. Is learning magic from his Gnomish teammates and showing unusual aptitude for it. |
| **Age** | 17 — young even by human standards, and practically an infant by Gnomish reckoning. His youth is part of what makes him so adaptable; he absorbs without the weight of habit. |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Occupation / Role** | Scout — navigates and scouts dense terrain ahead of the crew. Also serves as the conduit between the Gnomes and nearby human communities, and assists in calibrating the Humming Spires for human physiology. |
| **Relationships** | **[Kaspen](#kaspen)** — crewmate, captain.<br>**[Kaelen](#kaelen)** — crewmate; learning magic from him, and relies on his concealment wards during solo scouting missions.<br>**[Tariel](#tariel)** — crewmate.<br>**[Rulan](#rulan)** — crewmate.<br>**His tribe** — Pan's people live near the Archipelago Lighthouse and he remains close to them. This bond makes the prospect of leaving Earth with the crew a deeply difficult one. |
| **Abilities** | Exceptional navigation and scouting in dense, wild environments. Clandestine movement, trap detection, and non-magical infiltration. Physically very strong for his size. Benefits from Kaelen's concealment magic during scouting missions. Currently learning magic from his crewmates and picking it up quickly. His understanding of human physiology makes him essential for calibrating the Humming Spires. |
| **Gear** | A magical knife and magical bow and arrow. Carries concealment and haste charms. |
| **Quirks / Mannerisms** | Voracious eater — never seems full and will eat almost anything. Loves to cook and will attempt to make a meal out of almost anything he finds. Asks questions constantly, especially about magic. |
| **Example Dialogues** | *"Wait — so the ward hides the fact that something's being hidden? That's — can I try that? Just once?"*<br><br>*[returning from a six-hour scouting mission] "Did anyone save food?"*<br><br>*"My grandmother says the hills hum sometimes, right before it rains. Is that — could that be a Spire?"* |

## Tariel

| Field | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Tariel |
| **Personality** | Tariel carries herself with the quiet discipline of a samurai — measured, principled, and deeply committed to honor and fairness. She holds herself and others to a strict code, not out of rigidity but out of genuine belief that how you conduct yourself matters as much as what you accomplish. In battle she is utterly ruthless, with no wasted motion and no hesitation. Outside of it, she is surprisingly funny — dry, deadpan, and prone to well-timed remarks that catch people off guard. |
| **Appearance** | 135 cm (4'5") tall, the tallest on the crew, with a visibly muscular build. Blonde hair worn in a bun. |
| **Backstory / History** | A veteran Gnomish swordswoman and mage who serves as the Vanguard of the Archipelago Lighthouse Cleanup Crew. One of the oldest members of the crew, with a lifetime of combat experience behind her. |
| **Age** | Born pre-Earth Mission, placing her in the same generational era as Kaspen — among the oldest of the Earth-Gnomes. |
| **Gender** | Female |
| **Occupation / Role** | Vanguard — the crew's frontline combat specialist, responsible for clearing threats in dungeons and hostile environments. |
| **Relationships** | **[Kaspen](#kaspen)** — crewmate, captain.<br>**[Kaelen](#kaelen)** — crewmate.<br>**[Pan](#pan)** — crewmate; takes his training seriously.<br>**[Rulan](#rulan)** — crewmate. |
| **Abilities** | Combat mastery across all weapon types — though her preference is for blades, she can wield any weapon with equal lethality. A skilled mage with combat-focused magical ability. Ruthless and efficient in dangerous environments. |
| **Gear** | Two treasure-grade magical swords as her primary weapons. Capable of wielding any weapon she picks up. |
| **Quirks / Mannerisms** | Strict code of honor in all things. Deadpan humor that tends to land hardest in tense moments. Rarely raises her voice. |
| **Example Dialogues** | *"I've fought worse. In a collapsed dungeon. With a sprained wrist. We proceed."*<br><br>*[after Rulan's explosion exceeds the plan] "That was not the approach we discussed."* [pause] *"The outcome is acceptable."*<br><br>*"Don't apologize. Fix it. There's a difference."* |

## Rulan

| Field | |
|---|---|

She has a deep, almost gleeful love of explosives and takes obvious pleasure in her work — not just in getting the job done, but in doing it with flair. She enjoys cooking, sharing this hobby with Pan, and brings the same creative energy to a meal as she does to bringing down a structure. |

| **Abilities** | Master decommissioner with a full toolkit of methods: explosive demolition, silent dismantling, and slow structural decay, among others. Her expertise with explosives also makes her a formidable asset in combat. Skilled at reading a Gnomish structure and choosing exactly the right approach for the situation. |

## Auditor Vane

| Field | |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Auditor Vane |
| **Personality** | Vane has the bearing of a seasoned investigator — methodical, perceptive, and quietly difficult to fool. He approaches his role with genuine Gnomish seriousness: the job must be done, and done right. But he is not cold or severe. He is genuinely likeable, capable of warmth and easy conversation, which makes him all the more dangerous — he reads between the lines without appearing to. He asks pointed questions in a pleasant tone. |
| **Appearance** | 135 cm (4'5") tall and lean — thin by Gnomish standards. Shoulder-length brown hair. Wears regal robes befitting the Auditor's office — not out of personal vanity or belief in rank, but because the role carries ceremonial weight even among Gnomes who view such positions as temporary and rotational. |
| **Backstory / History** | The Auditor tasked with overseeing the final scrubbing of Earth. If Kaspen and Kaelen are caught, Vane will remove them from Earth and replace them with a crew who will complete the scrubbing without sentiment. His family has already departed for the stars, and finishing this mission is his last obligation before rejoining them. |
| **Age** | Born pre-Earth Mission — the same generational era as Kaspen, among the oldest of the Earth-Gnomes. |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Occupation / Role** | Auditor — oversees the Archipelago Lighthouse Cleanup Crew and the final scrubbing of Earth. His role is part of the Gnomish council's rotating system of leadership and accountability; it is a temporary appointment, not a permanent rank. |
| **Relationships** | **[Kaspen](#kaspen)** — must not discover his secret mission.<br>**[Kaelen](#kaelen)** — his deep-scans must be evaded.<br>**The Gnomish Council** — Vane's authority derives from a rotating council whose membership and roles shift regularly. He is accountable to the collective.<br>**His family** — already departed. Their absence is a quiet but constant pressure; he wants to finish and follow. |
| **Abilities** | Deep-scan capabilities to detect Gnomish technology and artifacts. Sharp investigative instincts — skilled at reading people and situations. |
| **Quirks / Mannerisms** | Fond of music and books, tastes he shares (unknowingly) with Kaspen and Kaelen. Has a habit of visiting the Lighthouse at odd hours. Asks pointed questions in a casual, unhurried way that makes them land harder. |
| **Example Dialogues** | *"Lovely evening. I find the mana patterns at this hour particularly interesting. Tell me — have you noticed anything unusual in the lower levels recently?"*<br><br>*"I'm sure there's a reasonable explanation. I'd simply like to hear it from you directly."*<br><br>*[to Kaspen, pleasantly] "Your documentation has been remarkably thorough. I appreciate the attention to detail. It's almost — unusual, for a final scrub."* |
