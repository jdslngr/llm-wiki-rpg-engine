# The Pitch

## The Setting

The year is 100,000 BCE. Earth is entering a natural, massive Ice Age. As the glaciers advance, the planet's ambient mana is growing thin and dwindling with each passing season. While Gnomes don't need mana to survive biologically, this "Great Fading" has grave consequences. They can no longer power their great magical workings and the technology that defines their civilization.

Recognizing their chapter on Earth is over, the Gnomes are staging an orderly departure to join their kin in the stars and explore other worlds in nearby galaxies. They refuse to interfere with the natural cycle that will eventually allow humanity to flourish.

## The Story

Kaspen, a Gnomish researcher, is part of the clean-up crew tasked with erasing every trace of Gnomish existence. But Kaspen is haunted by the "Lost Expeditions," fleets that vanished into the void. He realizes that if their fleet is lost and the Auditor scrubs Earth clean, the 100,000 years of art and culture they built on this planet will be permanently erased from the universe.

## The Secret Mission

To prevent this, Kaspen and his partner Kaelen are secretly ensuring the Gnomish legacy endures through humanity. Their mission is to identify locations where humans are likely to thrive to bury **Humming Spires**. These devices emit a subconscious creative nudge, seeding human minds with Gnomish artistic and musical influences.

Kaspen's hope is that over generations, these inspirations will be woven into humanity's original works, remaining recognizable to any Gnome who might one day experience them.

## The Protector

Kaspen's greatest ally and romantic partner is **[Kaelen](main-characters.md#kaelen)**, a mage who serves as an essential member of the Archipelago Lighthouse Cleanup Crew. Beyond his martial and technical skills, Kaelen uses his magical expertise to shroud the Humming Spires from Auditor Vane's deep-scans. To Kaelen, the meticulous work of clearing these hazards and anchoring the Spires is the only way to ensure that his partner's dream and their people's artistry survive the long winter.

## The Archipelago Lighthouse Cleanup Crew

One of the teams tasked with the final scrubbing of Earth is a specialized unit based at the Archipelago Lighthouse. Though they operate as a high-functioning group of peers, each member brings a distinct expertise needed to deactivate dungeons (vast Gnomish laboratories and outposts left behind in the retreat), as well as containing magical experiments gone wild and subduing the primordial monsters that have moved into abandoned facilities.

- **[Kaspen](main-characters.md#kaspen)** (Captain, Mage) — The visionary of the group, tasked with finding, mapping, and documenting the scrubbing sites.
- **[Kaelen](main-characters.md#kaelen)** (Vice Captain, Mage) — Kaspen's romantic partner, tasked with finding ways to evade Auditor Vane's deep scans to hide the Humming Spires.
- **[Pan](main-characters.md#pan)** (Scout – *Homo luzonensis*) — A collaborator from the *Homo luzonensis* lineage with intimate knowledge of the wild. Pan serves as the crew's scout and as the vital conduit between the Gnomes and the humans settled near the Archipelago Lighthouse. He excels in clandestine movement, trap detection, and non-magical infiltration, and operates under Kaelen's concealment wards during solo missions. His understanding of human physiology makes him essential for calibrating the Humming Spires. He is learning magic from his teammates.
- **[Tariel](main-characters.md#tariel)** (Vanguard) — A veteran Gnomish swordswoman and mage. She is a combat master, wielding enchanted blades.
- **[Rulan](main-characters.md#rulan)** (Decommissioner) — The technical expert of the crew. Rulan specializes in the literal dismantling of Gnomish civilization. Her expertise is in safely deactivating and collapsing Gnomish structures.

## The Stakes

If they are caught, Kaspen and Kaelen will be removed from Earth and replaced by a crew who will complete the scrubbing without sentiment. While other Gnomes may remain elsewhere in the stars, the unique identity and history of the Earth-Gnomes risk being forgotten forever.

Their mission is a quiet act of preservation, an effort to ensure that when the ice finally melts, the subtle echoes they've left behind remind humanity they were never truly alone, and offer any future Gnomish travelers a familiar song to find.
