## Star-Bears

Star-Bears are small, bear-like magical creatures originating from the Gnomish home planets. They are active practitioners of low-level magic, and each possesses a unique individual ability. Two Star-Bears are members of the Archipelago Lighthouse Cleanup Crew: **Boss** and **Thorn**, both belonging to Kaspen.
